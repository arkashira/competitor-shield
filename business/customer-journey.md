# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for Competitor Shield outlines the phases a SaaS business goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the platform's services. This map highlights trigger events, friction points, user emotions, opportunities to delight, and key metrics for each phase.

## Aware
### Trigger Event
- Discovery of a competitor mimicking their SaaS product or service through social media, industry reports, or word of mouth.
### Friction Points
- Difficulty in identifying and monitoring competitors.
- Lack of resources to dedicate to competitive intelligence.
### User Emotions
- Anxiety about losing market share.
- Frustration with the inability to stay ahead of competitors.
### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering a free trial or demo of Competitor Shield to showcase its capabilities.
- Providing educational content (blog posts, webinars) on the importance of competitive intelligence in the SaaS industry.
### Metric
- **Awareness Rate**: The percentage of SaaS businesses that become aware of Competitor Shield out of the total potential market.

## Consider
### Trigger Event
- Realization of the need for a systematic approach to competitive intelligence after experiencing losses due to competitors.
### Friction Points
- Evaluating the cost-benefit analysis of investing in a competitive intelligence platform.
- Comparing Competitor Shield with other similar solutions in the market.
### User Emotions
- Curiosity about how Competitor Shield can address their specific pain points.
- Skepticism about the effectiveness and ROI of such a platform.
### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering case studies or testimonials from existing clients.
- Providing a comprehensive comparison guide of Competitor Shield versus other solutions.
### Metric
- **Consideration Rate**: The percentage of aware SaaS businesses that consider purchasing Competitor Shield out of those who are aware.

## Try
### Trigger Event
- Decision to sign up for a free trial or a pilot project to test Competitor Shield's features and benefits.
### Friction Points
- Technical difficulties during the onboarding process.
- Initial confusion with the platform's interface or features.
### User Emotions
- Excitement about the potential benefits of using Competitor Shield.
- Apprehension about the learning curve and integration with existing systems.
### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering personalized onboarding support and training.
- Providing immediate value through actionable insights during the trial period.
### Metric
- **Trial Conversion Rate**: The percentage of trial users who convert to paid customers out of those who start a trial.

## Adopt
### Trigger Event
- Purchase of a subscription to Competitor Shield after a successful trial or pilot.
### Friction Points
- Integration challenges with existing workflows and tools.
- Need for ongoing support and training for optimal use.
### User Emotions
- Satisfaction with the decision to invest in Competitor Shield.
- Relief that they are now better equipped to handle competitive threats.
### Opportunities to Delight
- Regular updates with new features and enhancements based on user feedback.
- Excellent customer support to ensure high user satisfaction.
### Metric
- **Customer Retention Rate**: The percentage of customers who continue their subscription over a specified period.

## Expand
### Trigger Event
- Realization of the full value of Competitor Shield and the desire to expand its use within the organization or to other products/services.
### Friction Points
- Justifying the cost of expanding the service to stakeholders.
- Technical challenges in scaling the platform's use.
### User Emotions
- Loyalty to Competitor Shield as a trusted solution.
- Enthusiasm about exploring more advanced features or premium services.
### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering tiered pricing plans that incentivize expansion.
- Developing strategic partnerships to enhance the platform's capabilities.
### Metric
- **Expansion Rate**: The percentage of existing customers who expand their use of Competitor Shield over a specified period.

By understanding and addressing the needs, emotions, and friction points at each phase of the customer journey, Competitor Shield can tailor its marketing, sales, and support efforts to provide a superior customer experience, ultimately driving growth and customer loyalty.
