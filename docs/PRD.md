# Product Requirements Document: Competitor Shield
## Problem Statement
In today's fast-paced and competitive market, businesses face numerous threats from their competitors. These threats can range from new product launches to aggressive marketing campaigns, and if not addressed promptly, can lead to significant losses in market share and revenue. The current process of monitoring competitors is often manual, time-consuming, and prone to errors, making it challenging for businesses to respond effectively to competitive threats.

## Target Users
The target users of Competitor Shield are business professionals responsible for monitoring and responding to competitive threats, including:

* Product managers
* Marketing managers
* Competitive intelligence analysts
* Business development managers

## Goals
The primary goal of Competitor Shield is to provide an AI-driven alert system that enables businesses to detect and respond to competitive threats in a timely and effective manner. The specific goals of the project are:

* To develop an AI-powered system that can monitor competitors and detect potential threats
* To provide actionable insights and recommendations for users to respond to threats
* To improve the efficiency and effectiveness of competitive threat detection and response

## Key Features
The following are the key features of Competitor Shield, prioritized based on their importance and complexity:

1. **Threat Detection**: Develop an AI-powered algorithm that can monitor competitors and detect potential threats, such as new product launches, changes in pricing or marketing strategies, and other competitive activities.
2. **Alert System**: Design an alert system that notifies users of detected threats, providing them with timely and relevant information to respond to competitive challenges.
3. **Insight Generation**: Develop a feature that provides users with actionable insights and recommendations for responding to competitive threats, such as market analysis, customer feedback, and competitor profiling.
4. **User Notification**: Implement a user notification system that sends alerts and insights to users via email, SMS, or other preferred communication channels.
5. **Threat Management**: Develop a threat management system that allows users to track, prioritize, and respond to competitive threats, including features for assigning tasks, setting deadlines, and monitoring progress.

## Success Metrics
The success of Competitor Shield will be measured based on the following metrics:

* **Detection Accuracy**: The accuracy of the AI-powered algorithm in detecting competitive threats
* **Response Time**: The time it takes for users to respond to competitive threats after receiving alerts and insights
* **User Engagement**: The level of user engagement with the system, including the number of users, frequency of use, and user satisfaction
* **Business Impact**: The impact of Competitor Shield on business outcomes, including revenue growth, market share, and customer acquisition

## Scope
The scope of Competitor Shield includes:

* Developing an AI-powered threat detection algorithm
* Designing an alert system and user notification feature
* Generating actionable insights and recommendations for users
* Implementing a threat management system
* Integrating with existing business systems and tools

## Out-of-Scope
The following features and functionalities are out of scope for the initial release of Competitor Shield:

* Integration with social media platforms
* Support for multiple languages
* Advanced analytics and reporting features
* Customizable dashboards and user interfaces

By focusing on the key features and functionalities outlined in this PRD, Competitor Shield aims to provide a comprehensive and effective solution for businesses to detect and respond to competitive threats, ultimately driving business growth and success.
